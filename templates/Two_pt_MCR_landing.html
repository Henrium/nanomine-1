This page allows you to characterize and recontruct images of polymer nanocomposites. Please click on the appropriate button.

STEP 1: Image Binarization

Characterization and Reconstruction algorithms work with binarized images. If you want to binarize grayscale SEM/TEM images, use our Niblack Binarizarion webtool.

STEP 2: Image Characterization

Upload a binarized microstructure image / set of images and obtain characterization data of your choice.

STEP 3: Image Reconstruction

Upload a binary microstructure image / set of images and obtain 2D/3D reconstructions using method of your choice.

Reference

MD Rintoul and S Torquato, Reconstruction of the structure of dispersions, Journal of Colloid and Interface Science, 186, 467-476 (1997)

Liu, Y., Greene, M.S., Chen, W., Dikin, D., Liu, W.K., Computational Microstructure Characterization and Reconstruction for Stochastic Multiscale Design, Computer Aided Design, 45 (1), 65-76, 2013
